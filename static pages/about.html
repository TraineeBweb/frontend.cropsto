About CropSto: Global Leaders in Sustainable Packaging

  • Pioneering Innovation in Hermetic Storage Solutions

    CropSto is not just a brand—it is a global force reshaping the future of agricultural storage and preservation. As a leader in high-barrier hermetic packaging, we provide cutting-edge solutions that safeguard the freshness and quality of green coffee, cocoa, tea, sugar, rice, spices, maize, and grains—ensuring they reach markets in peak condition, free from contamination and deterioration.

  • Excellence Rooted in Legacy

    A proud member of the JMT Group, CropSto inherits a rich legacy of agro-commodity trading and stockholding dating back to 1976. Our foundation is built on decades of expertise, a commitment to innovation, and an unwavering focus on quality. Headquartered in Rajkot, Gujarat, India, we operate at the intersection of tradition and technology, ensuring that every solution we provide meets the highest industry standards.

  • A Global Presence, A Local Commitment

    With a strategic global distribution network, CropSto ensures seamless availability of our advanced hermetic storage solutions across Central America, South America, Africa, Asia, and beyond. Our presence in key agricultural hubs allows us to work closely with farmers, traders, and exporters—delivering localized expertise with a world-class approach.

  • Unmatched Quality, Recognized Worldwide

    CropSto is not just a supplier; we are a benchmark in quality. As an esteemed member of the Federation of Indian Export Organizations (FIEO) —a prestigious body established by the Ministry of Commerce, Government of India —we adhere to the highest international quality and safety standards. Our commitment to sustainability and eco-conscious innovation has positioned us as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide.

  • Sustainability at the Core

    At CropSto, sustainability is not an afterthought—it’s our foundation. Our hermetic packaging solutions eliminate the need for harmful pesticides and preservatives, reduce post-harvest losses, and promote environmentally responsible storage practices. By advancing recyclable, reusable, and high-performance packaging, we contribute to a greener, more sustainable future for global agriculture.

  • Join the CropSto Movement

    CropSto is not just a company—it’s a revolution in agricultural preservation. We are redefining the way crops are stored, transported, and protected, bridging the gap between innovation, sustainability, and global food security. Partner with us and experience the future of post-harvest storage—where technology meets sustainability to shape a fresher, safer world.
