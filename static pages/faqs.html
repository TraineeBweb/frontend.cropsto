Check out our Frequently Asked Questions.

What is hermetic packaging, and how does it work?

Hermetic packaging is an advanced sealing technology that creates an airtight barrier, effectively shielding stored contents from external elements such as oxygen, carbon dioxide, and moisture. This helps maintain product freshness and extends shelf life without the need for chemical preservatives.

Are CropSto bags made from food-grade materials?

Yes, all CropSto bags are manufactured using food-grade materials and comply with stringent international safety regulations, including USFDA and EU standards. Certification from SGS can be provided upon request.

What is the lifespan of CropSto Bags?

CropSto Bags are designed for durability and can be effectively used for up to five years from the date of manufacture.

How long do food grains remain fresh in CropSto Bags?

Our multi-layer, high-barrier packaging technology preserves food grains, including coffee beans, for more than three times their typical shelf life without requiring pesticides or insecticides, even in varying climatic conditions.

What does "multi-layer high-barrier" mean in CropSto packaging?

CropSto bags are constructed from multiple layers of high-barrier, recyclable plastic resins that provide superior oxygen and moisture resistance. This ensures extended freshness for stored grains and enhances protection against environmental factors.

What is the thickness of CropSto packaging films?

CropSto packaging films have a consistent thickness profile with minimal variation. Our storage bags are 80–82 microns thick, while our vacuum bags, FIBC liners, and sample bags have a thickness of 150 microns.

Are CropSto bags certified as per international standards?

Yes, CropSto Bags meet US FDA and EU compliance standards for food-grade safety. Certification documents can be shared upon request.

What are the standard packaging dimensions and weight of CropSto products?

Detailed packaging specifications, including box dimensions and weight, can be found in our product catalog or price list.

How long can green coffee beans retain moisture and aroma in CropSto Bags?

When properly packed and stored, green coffee beans retain their moisture and aroma for over a year due to the high-barrier properties of CropSto Bags.

Can CropSto Bags be reused?

Yes, our storage bags can be reused at least three times when handled and stored correctly.

How are CropSto Storage Bags sealed?

They can be securely sealed using cable ties or heat-sealing machines, depending on the specific packaging application.

Is it possible to heat-seal CropSto Bags?

Yes, a heat-sealing machine of the appropriate width can be used to hermetically seal CropSto Bags for added protection.

How does CropSto packaging help preserve coffee aroma and moisture?

The oxygen-barrier properties of our bags ensure that the natural aroma remains locked inside, while moisture-barrier technology prevents humidity from affecting the stored grains.

Do you offer vacuum sealing machines for CropSto Vacuum Bags?

Yes, we provide high-performance vacuum sealing machines, such as the Vacupack VD-700, capable of sealing up to 20 kg of food grains efficiently.

What is the storage capacity of CropSto FIBC Bags for green coffee beans?

Storage capacity depends on the bulk density of the green coffee beans, but our 1-ton (1T) FIBC bags can safely hold up to 1000 kg of green beans.

What sets CropSto products apart from competitors?

CropSto offers multi-layered, high-barrier, transparent, and recyclable food-grade plastic bags with near-zero oxygen and moisture transmission rates. This ensures prolonged storage life, superior aroma retention, and optimal food safety.

What secondary packaging options work best with CropSto Storage Bags?

For added protection, handling, and stacking convenience, CropSto Storage Bags can be placed inside jute or polypropylene bags.

Are reusable cable ties available for sealing CropSto Bags?

Yes, we provide durable, reusable cable ties to securely close our storage bags.

What is the ideal moisture level for dried cocoa and green coffee beans when using CropSto Bags?

The recommended moisture content is:

  • 7–8% for dried cocoa beans
  • 10–12% for green coffee beans
What are the ideal temperature and humidity conditions for storing grains in CropSto Bags?

For best results, store grains at a temperature between 15°C to 30°C and maintain relative humidity between 40% to 70%. These conditions minimize condensation risks and help maintain the quality of the stored produce.

Are CropSto Bags recyclable and reusable?

Yes, CropSto Bags are 100% recyclable and reusable. Our commitment to sustainability ensures that our packaging solutions minimize environmental impact while maintaining product integrity. Learn more about our recycling initiatives here.

Are CropSto Bags eco-friendly?

Absolutely! Sustainability is at the core of CropSto’s mission. We prioritize eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient processes, and waste-reduction strategies to protect our planet while delivering high-performance packaging solutions.

Are CropSto Bags biodegradable?

Currently, our bags are not biodegradable. However, they are designed for multiple reuses and can be fully recycled to reduce waste and environmental impact.
