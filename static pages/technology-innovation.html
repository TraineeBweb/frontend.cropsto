Technology and Innovation

Next-Generation Hermetic Storage: Precision-Engineered for Maximum Protection

  • Advanced Food-Grade Polymer Engineering

    CropSto’s hermetic storage solutions are crafted using scientifically formulated food-grade polymer blends designed for superior strength, barrier protection, and compliance with global food safety standards. Our materials are selected based on their:

    Ultra-Low Oxygen Transmission Rate (OTR): Restricts oxygen ingress, preventing oxidation and microbial growth.

    Minimal Moisture Vapor Transmission Rate (MVTR): Ensures humidity control to prevent mold, spoilage, and mycotoxin formation.

    High Tensile Strength & Puncture Resistance: Enhances durability, making it suitable for long-term bulk storage.

    Non-Toxic & BPA-Free Composition : Ensures zero contamination risk for food-grade storage applications.

  • Multi-Layered Hermetic Barrier Technology

    Our proprietary multi-layer co-extrusion process integrates high-performance polymer films, ensuring a robust defense against environmental threats:

    Outer Structural Layer : Provides mechanical strength and resistance to external damage.

    Gas Barrier Layer : Engineered with high-barrier polymers to restrict oxygen permeation and maintain a controlled atmosphere.

    Moisture-Blocking Layer : Prevents humidity fluctuations, reducing condensation risks inside the packaging.

    Inner Food-Contact Layer : Composed of certified food-grade materials to maintain purity and safety.

    This precision-layering technology ensures long-term food preservation without the need for chemical treatments or synthetic fumigants.

  • Hermetic Sealing & Controlled Atmosphere Technology

    CropSto’s advanced hermetic sealing technology eliminates the possibility of gas leaks, creating a stable anaerobic environment that actively protects stored commodities:

    Modified Atmospheric Equilibrium : Our bags naturally regulate internal gases, increasing CO₂ concentration while reducing oxygen levels. This inhibits insect proliferation, mold growth, and mycotoxin formation.

    blogs Seal Integrity Testing : Every batch undergoes rigorous pressure and gas retention tests to validate long-term hermetic performance.

    Adaptive Storage Mechanism : Designed to work seamlessly across different climatic conditions, from humid tropics to arid environments, ensuring reliable performance in diverse global supply chains.

    By integrating cutting-edge material science, atmospheric control, and seal integrity validation, CropSto delivers a next-generation storage solution that ensures maximum efficiency, sustainability, and food safety compliance.

  • Driving Innovation for a Sustainable Future

    CropSto continues to push the boundaries of hermetic storage innovation, developing next-gen solutions that:

    ✔ Reduce post-harvest losses : Minimizes wastage by up to 40%, ensuring higher yield retention.

    ✔ Chemical-Free Preservation : Eliminates the need for fumigants and preservatives, promoting safer storage.

    ✔ Extended Shelf Life : Controls oxygen and moisture levels to maintain product freshness for longer durations.

    ✔ Sustainable & Recyclable : Supports a circular economy with eco-friendly material options.

Join us in transforming agricultural storage through precision-engineered technology and sustainable innovation.
